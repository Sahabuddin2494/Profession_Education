About The Company

We thrive because of your dreams..

Apollo International is a pioneer name in education and migration services in Australia and around the world. We provide detailed information on all aspects of study abroad from applying for scholarships, to how to get visas, courses offered at universities, living costs.

We started our business in 2017 with a small team. We were dedicated to providing the best possible client support and saw tremendous growth in recent years as a result. Our clients are the lifeblood of our business and we would do anything to make sure they’re happy. We continue to work hard every day to make sure we provide the best experience for them.

With 9 branches around the world, we are a trusted name in admission and visa counseling, with years of experience in helping students find the right school for them. At Apollo International, we offer a variety of counseling services for students looking to study abroad. We can help you with everything from visa applications to school selection, making the process easy and hassle-free. Thank you for being part of our journey!
